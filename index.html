My name is Sanya Chhabra and I am a 3rd year Biology major and pre-dental student at University of California, Riverside. My hobbies include cooking, baking, photography, and watching TV shows. I am currently involved in volunteering at Riverside Community Hospital where I restock supplies in the emergency room, perform clerical tasks such as answering phone calls, delivering files to appropriate departments, assembling paperwork packets, and escort patients around the hospital. I have been volunteering there since January of 2023.

Another thing I am involved in is CNAS Scholars in which I am a Peer Mentor. CNAS Scholars is a program which is part of the College of Natural and Agricultural Sciences Undergraduate Academic Advising Center and it aims to help first year students by putting them in learning communities and giving them the opportunity to sign up to have a Peer Mentor. As a Peer Mentor, I meet with students twice a quarter and answer their questions. I also have presented at workshops and events geared toward helping freshmen adjust to college, co-instructed NASC093 freshman advising course; planned lessons and monitored attendance, monitored and responded to student questions and concerns on peer advising email account, designed flyers for CNAS Scholars social media, and hosted drop-in peer advising hours. I was also a Peer Advisor last year and I am currently a Peer Mentor.

One of my other extracurricular activities is Public Health Peers which is part of The Well at UC Riverside in which I am a marketing committee member. My job is to create social media posts about a variety of public health topics, distribute informational flyers around campus, and assist in preparation for events promoting student health. I have been a volunteer since January of 2023. Another extracurricular I have been a part of is the American Cancer Society West Region Campus Leadership Team in which I was the Recognition Chair from July 2021 to June 2022. I led activities and process to identify and then recognize various American Cancer Society (ACS) on Campus chapters for excellence in leadership and fundraising, wrote newsletter articles publicizing ACS initiatives, collaborated with high school and college campuses in West region to share ideas, resources & strategies, and designed and posted flyers on social media.
